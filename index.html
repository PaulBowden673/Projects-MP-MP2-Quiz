Health & Safety Quiz Rules

Answer as many of the questions as you can. The game will end once all of the questions have been answered.

How To Play:

  • Press Play to start the quiz game
  • Once the game starts, you will be presented with a question, select one of the 4 options to answer the question
  • If you answer correctly, the selected answer will turn green, if answered incorrectly this will turn red
  • For every correct answer, the score will increase by 10 points, incorrect answers get 0 points
  • Once the question is answered, you will be presented with the next question, same steps as above apply
  • Your progress is recorded in the top left
  • You can end the game anytime by selecting Quit Game
  • Once all the questions have been answered, the quiz game will end
  • You can save Your score by entering a username and clicking Save Score
  • Click Play Again to start the quiz again
  • Click Return Home to go back to the home screen
  • To view the highscores table, select Highscores, only the top 10 highest scores will be displayed
  • To clear the highscores table of recorded scores, click Clear Scores
